Travel to Chiang Mai Thailand

Doi Inthanon National Park Chiang Mai Thailand

About

Chiang Mai is the largest city in northern Thailand and the second largest city in Thailand.

Things to Do

You can visit the national park called Doi Inthanon National Park and hike the highest mountain in Thailand called Doi Inthanon.

Vegan Cuisine

Chiang Mai is considered the vegan capital of Asia. There are many Thai and international vegan restaurants and dishes available in the city.
